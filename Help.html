Help Center

Our goal is to provide a seamless shopping experience with fast delivery and reliable customer support.

For any assistance regarding your orders or account, our team is always here to help you promptly.

1. How to place an order?

Select your product → Tap the Order button → Fill the form → Your order will be submitted instantly.

2. What is the delivery time?

Delivery takes 3–5 days on average, depending on your city or location.

3. How does payment work?

We offer both Cash on Delivery (COD) and Advance Payment options. Pay only when you receive your product with COD, or pay securely online in advance.

4. How can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order from the My Orders page if it is not shipped yet.

5. Need further assistance?

If you face any issue related to orders, deliveries, or your account, you can contact our support team anytime.
